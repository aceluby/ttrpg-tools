Last updated: 2026-03-15

This file captures recurring NPCs, factions, loyalties, and hidden motives.

## Prince Dorian Vaelor

- Role: prince of `Everthrone`, trusted ally of the party
- Public face: stoic, dutiful, and focused on responsibility over comfort
- Known strengths:
  - takes duty seriously
  - uses the `weave` to protect his people
- Current pressures:
  - tied to the political marriage with the `Zephandor` princess
  - caught in a cold rivalry with `Rhaegar`
- Party view:
  - the party sees him as a trusted ally
- Threat context:
  - protects his people from the `Red Reavers`, the northern raiding parties
- Family dynamic:
  - sees `Rhaegar` as a little brother even though `Rhaegar` hates him

## Nyx

  - to the point
- Loyalty:

- Open details:

## Nico Vale

- Role:
  - information broker tied to the assassin world
- Public face:
  - charming
  - easy to underestimate
  - seems more like a gossiping fixer than a serious threat
- Core drive:
  - sells information to whoever pays
- Trade specialty:
  - everything
  - court gossip
  - servant chatter
  - shipping and market movement
  - military rumor
  - affair blackmail
  - street disappearances
  - assassin-adjacent secrets
- Hidden method:
  - secretly maintains a network of child informants
  - these children move through markets, alleys, kitchens, stables, shrines, and noble service spaces without drawing notice
  - Nico uses them to gather fragments that adults dismiss as harmless
- Loyalty:
  - fluid
- Faction lens:
  - `Ugly` within the king's assassins
- Threat value:
  - useful to every side and loyal to none by default
- Strengths:
  - hears things early
  - turns scraps into leverage
  - rarely appears to be the source of what he knows
- Weakness:
  - his network is powerful but morally dangerous; if exposed, it would make him a target for every faction at once
- Current ethical line:
  - he is willing to use children as eyes and ears
  - he is less willing to knowingly send them into direct lethal danger
  - whether that line holds under pressure remains uncertain
- Social style:
  - trades in half-smiles, selective honesty, and the feeling that he always knows slightly more than he says
- Open details:
  - how he recruits or protects the children in his network
  - whether he sees himself as protecting them, exploiting them, or both
  - who currently has the most leverage over him
