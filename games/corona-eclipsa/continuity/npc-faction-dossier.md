## Prince Dorian Vaelor

- Role: prince of `Everthrone`, trusted ally of the party
- Public face: stoic, dutiful, and focused on responsibility over comfort
- Physical or behavioral trait:
  - gives a slight upward nod before speaking, as if confirming his own resolve first
- Known strengths:
  - takes duty seriously
  - uses the `weave` to protect his people
- Current pressures:
  - tied to the political marriage with the `Zephandor` princess
  - caught in a cold rivalry with `Rhaegar`
- Party view:
  - the party sees him as a trusted ally
- Threat context:
  - protects his people from the `Red Reavers`, the northern raiding parties
- Family dynamic:
  - sees `Rhaegar` as a little brother even though `Rhaegar` hates him

## Queen Rosamund Vaelor

- Role: queen of `Everthrone`, mother of `Rhaegar`
- Public face:
  - gracious
  - immaculately composed

- Faction lens:
  - `Bad` within the Everthrone court

## Princess Serel Aiuvarin

- Role: royal figure tied to the `Dorian` marriage alliance
- Public face: politically serious and defined by duty to her people
- Physical or behavioral trait:
  - speaks fairly quickly, especially when she has already decided what must be done
- Cultural note:
  - known among her people as `Sacrifice`
- Core drive:
  - honestly wants what is best for her people
  - will sacrifice almost anything, including herself, to secure that future
  - now sees the people of `Everthrone` as part of that responsibility through her marriage to `Dorian`
- Moral center:
  - one of the most genuinely good people in the campaign
  - not naive, but fundamentally oriented toward protection rather than personal power
- Political function:
  - central to the alliance proving `Zephandor` was not behind the staged attacks
- Family notes:
  - daughter of `King Therion Aiuvarin` and `Queen Lirael Aiuvarin`
  - sister of the late `Prince Kaelith Aiuvarin`

## Prince Rhaegar Vaelor

- Role: prince of `Everthrone`, active antagonist
- Public face: charismatic and aloof
- Physical or behavioral trait:
  - speaks with his hands constantly, as if every word deserves a stage
- True drive:
  - wants to become king
  - acts from narcissism and does not care about larger consequences
- Relationship to the party:
  - hates the group

  - had `Prince Kaelith Aiuvarin` killed and arranged for the party to be blamed
- Political position:
  - secretly works with `Cerres`

- Family dynamic:
  - hates `Dorian`
