# Corona Eclipsa Custom Rules And Procedures

Last updated: 2026-03-13

This file captures table-specific mechanics, procedures, and rulings that differ from baseline Pathfinder 2e or materially affect session prep.

## Dungeon Turns

- Procedure:
  - dungeon crawls use dungeon turns
- Turn length:
  - 10 minutes
- Turn scope:
  - moving to a new area is usually one turn
  - searching a room is usually one turn
  - forcing a door is usually one turn
  - treating wounds is usually one turn
  - working through a puzzle step is usually one turn
  - broadly, anything that takes more than a combat action consumes a turn
- Purpose:
  - supports structured exploration, movement, investigation, and pressure in dungeon play
- Encounter pressure:
  - random encounters follow dungeon initiative
  - most stretches call for a `d6` random encounter roll
  - a result of `1` means an encounter
- Open details:
  - whether specific dungeons alter the encounter rate

## Stealth And Surprise

- Ambush rule:
  - if a character is stealthed and not noticed, they can take a free opening shot
  - that opening shot can include sneak attack when appropriate
- Transition to initiative:
  - once everyone is aware the action is happening, initiative is rolled
- Initiative modifier:
  - the ambushing group gets `+2` to initiative rolls
- Surprise advantage:
  - the ambushing group also gains any applicable surprise-round style benefits used at the table
