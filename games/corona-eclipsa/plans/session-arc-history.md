# Session Arc History

Last updated: 2026-03-13

This file compresses the campaign into major arcs rather than session-by-session notes.

## Arc 1: The First Fall

- Start point:
  - the party's first autumn together
  - escorting the merchant `Ames` from `Vaelport` to `Everthrone` as he expanded his business there
- Setup:
  - `Ames` was a merchant who happened to know all the characters and asked for their help
- Early conflict:
  - the group was attacked by bandits who appeared to be from `Zephandor`
  - the attack was actually a false flag operation
- Everthrone turn:
  - once in `Everthrone`, the party received a mysterious letter from `Nyx`
  - `Nyx` tested them personally, then recruited them into the kingdom's covert work
  - their first major secret-hand job was infiltrating `Lord Carston Rhyne`'s manor and robbing a hidden vault
  - during this stretch they also met `Kristopher`, first crossed paths with `Vivacia`, and began uncovering Harlan's beast-magic thread
- Rhyne fallout:
  - the party later moved to intercept `Lord Carston Rhyne` but found him already dead
  - his ledger tied him to `Rhaegar` and a mysterious outside funding source
  - surviving bandits and medallions pointed toward `Zephandor`
- Major event:
  - at `Iron Hollow`, the party found the entire town transformed into zombie-like creatures
  - this condition is known as `hollowed`
  - the party killed the entire town
  - they became secretly known as the `Butchers of Iron Hollow`
- Royal escalation:
  - `Princess Serel Aiuvarin` proposed peace through marriage
  - a surviving dock boy described the fall of `Iron Hollow` to the crown
  - the party was sent as royal envoys to `Zephandor`
- Journey discovery:
  - on the road to `Zephandor`, the party discovered a purpose-built silent dungeon tied to a dead dragon and dragon keepers
  - Dax gained an amulet that grants an ephemeral dragon mount that can be ridden but not flown
  - the group recovered dragon-linked items, silver, and visions that deepened several character arcs
- Zephandor crisis:
  - when the party arrived in `Zephandor`, `Rhaegar` was already there being received
  - `Prince Kaelith Aiuvarin` was killed
  - the party was framed for the murder
  - Harlan survived `Rhaegar`'s direct attempt on his life in the bathhouse
  - the party eventually cleared their name with `Serel`
  - despite her brother's death, `Princess Serel Aiuvarin` remained determined to proceed with the marriage
- End point:
  - the arc closes with the party returning toward `Everthrone` carrying the peace proposal and the truth that `Rhaegar` was behind `Kaelith`'s death
- Major outcomes:
  - the group survived the first kingdom-level conspiracy
  - `Nighteyes` was released and bonded with `Harlan`
  - Jin was named champion by `Soryan`
  - Marcel's prophetic and changeling threads intensified
- Hidden truth:
  - `Rhaegar` had `Prince Kaelith Aiuvarin` killed

## Arc 2: The Wedding And The Tournament

- Start point:
  - after returning from `Zephandor`, the party pushed to keep the peace plan alive inside `Everthrone`
- Core drive:
  - secure the alliance before `Rhaegar` or court forces could break it
- Major developments:
  - the party convinced `Prince Dorian` to marry `Princess Serel Aiuvarin`
  - the alliance between `Everthrone` and `Zephandor` was secured despite sabotage and bloodshed
  - the group then spent two sessions in a tournament during the harvest festival
  - `Corona Eclipsa` won the tournament
  - as part of their victory, the party was granted `Lord Carston Rhyne`'s manor
- Arc value:
  - the party gained status, property, and a stronger foothold in the kingdom they had been serving from the shadows
- End point:
  - the wedding and tournament success left the party established inside `Everthrone` before the next major lull

## Arc 3: Six Months Of Downtime

- Start point:
  - after winning `Lord Carston Rhyne`'s manor, the party entered roughly six months of downtime
- Purpose:
  - consolidate gains, study, train, craft, and let long-term political consequences ripen
- Arc value:
  - this stretch helped transition the campaign from the first kingdom-crisis arc into the current deeper dragon-and-hollowed storyline
- End point:
  - the party emerged with new clues leading them away from `Everthrone` and toward `Briarpatch`

## Arc 4: The Search For A Weapon

- Current status:
  - this is the current arc
- Core drive:
  - the party is looking for a weapon capable of fighting the `hollowed`
- Start point:
  - after six months of downtime, the party followed clues to the town of `Briarpatch`
  - in `Briarpatch`, they found notes leading to a dragon shrine
  - at the shrine, they found an unguarded artifact with powerful magic
- Major complication:
  - when they touched the artifact, they were whisked into a pocket dimension where time passes much faster than outside
  - `the Corterie` was already there in a heated battle even though they had been days behind the party
- Inside-dimension realization:
  - the time mismatch suggests the pocket dimension heavily distorts time
  - the being trapped inside has been there for hundreds of years in real time
- Known future consequence:
  - when the party escapes, months will have passed in the outside world
  - `Dorian` will be gone
  - the king will be dead
  - `Rhaegar` will be trying to seize control
  - `Princess Serel Aiuvarin` will resist him
  - fearing for her life, she will insist on searching for `Dorian`
- Prophetic setup:
  - Marcel has already dreamed of this outcome
- Planned climax:
  - `Dorian` will carve his own dragon to fight the wizards and bandits harrying the party
- Open details:
  - what happens inside the pocket dimension before escape
  - who the wizards and bandits are at the arc climax
  - what choice the party will make about the trapped wizard

### Arc 4 Hidden Truth

- The "weapon" is most likely the trapped wizard himself.
- He trapped himself in the pocket dimension by accident.
- If released, he wants to live forever.
- He is not tied to `Cerres`, `Rhaegar`, or another known faction.
- He should not be set free.

### Arc 4 Character Beat

- `Nighteyes` once called Harlan `packless`.
- He now calls him `changer` or `brother`, marking a deeper stage in their bond.

- Planned focus:
  - the party will search near `Vaelport` for Dax's father
- Start trigger:
  - Dax had previously written to his father and searched for him without success
  - after the Arc 4 time lapse, Dax will learn that his father sent a letter
  - the letter points to the dangerous caves
  - Dax's father is searching there for Dax's mother, whom Dax assumes is dead
- Major route:
  - a long and dangerous tunnel
- Major reveal:
  - the tunnel will lead to the first living dragon
- Central uncertainty:

  - what role dragonblood destiny plays in the arc

## Session-By-Session Recaps

### The Fall of Iron Hollow

A group of guards and the old alchemist hear a scream from the docks and investigate. There they find a dead body and the Vraegari (northern half-orc/viking) mysteriously showing up out of nowhere in an attack. After dealing with the raiders and investigating, they find a medallion on one of the attackers, when the alarm bells start ringing. After a hard fight the heroes were captured, tied, and were forced into a mysterious ritual where the entire group fades into a chorus of screams.

### Session 1 - Travel to Everthrone

Jin Sakura, Daxon Wraxor, Harlan Corby, and Axin Svalison agree to help a Vaelport vendor set up shop in Everthrone. About 6 hours away they were stopped by travelers who tried to extort the group. Not wanting to fight, Daxon tried very hard for a non-violent approach, however once the first arrow was loosed, the game was over and Daxon nearly decapitated the leader while the rest of the bandits were scattered. Upon entering Everthrone the PCs are invited to wait for Ames at the Thirsty Minstrel, where they meet Wynna the waitress and a mysterious letter is dropped for the players.

> Heard about your little scuffle. Meet at the abandoned house near the castle tonight.

### Session 2 - The Letter

After receiving the letter the group decides to wait for Ames for payment and were given more than agreed to for their travels (50 gp each). He points them in the direction of the house mentioned in the letter and tells them of its haunting, where a man went crazy with the beast magic and killed his entire family. On the way to the house, they pass a kennel where a pup reached out and spoke to Harlan, which seemed to anger the kennel master as he took the dog by the scruff and locked him out back, away from his littermates. When the group finally made its way to the house, they found a dilapidated junk pile of a home with a single inhabitant, Nyx, who immediately attacked the group. Nyx focused on non-damaging combat, using tricks like tripping, readying actions as reactions, using difficult terrain and corners to her advantage, and hiding behind enemies for cover against other enemies. It was a strong tactical lesson that ended with her being satisfied enough with how the group operates. The session ended with a proposal of a long-term job, one where the group would be handling some of the more unsavory bits of running a kingdom, working for the king as his secret hand, with strong promises of future wealth and comfort. The group agreed and took the first job: infiltrate the manor of Lord Carsten Rhyne, break into his underground safe, and steal a very particular intricate box.

### Session 3 - The Concert

The group was given a task by Nyx to infiltrate Lord Carsten Rhyn's manor. They must get into his cellar, find the hidden vault, go in and steal an intricate box from the vault. They have a few days to complete this.

The players decided to head to the Noble District to scope out the manor, but on the way Marcel tried singing a tune just as the houses became nicer and guards became more prevalent. Some guards were impressed, others were not and caused a bit of a scene. They made their way to Carsten Rhyne's manor and found a typically fortified home with a single guard on duty, a guard that due to the ruckus caused down the street and the obvious traveling clothes by the party, was on high alert, staring down the company. They were able to see what looks like the owner of the home through a window place a necklace into a bedside table. They were also able to see a kitchen area through a window, but more of a kitchen where the help prepared their food, not the normal kitchen where they prepare food for the lord.

Not wanting to blow their cover, they dropped the infiltration and went to the Thirsty Minstrel where they convinced Wynna that they are expected to play music tonight. They have a great show, with Jin doing an interpretive dance while Marcel plays, Dax acts as hype man, and Harlan scopes out any folks who look out of place. They get a free room for the night, nothing happens overnight and the party awakens fresh and ready for the day at 8 am.

### Session 4 - The Setup

Marcel awakes to a disturbing dream where one voice tells him to seek the catalyst, while a mysterious woman tells him not to. In this dream were images of dragons, burning cities, and fire. He told the group about these dreams and immediately went to the church to see if there were any books on dragons.

On the way to the church the group upgraded Jin's staff to a +1 staff of returning, and Dax's axe to a +1 axe. Upon entering the church they see a priest, who tells them that they have no books on dragons, but that there are some historical books in the castle. If they can convince Nyx to bring them on as the king's assassins, they should have better access to the king's library.

Needing to spend some time waiting for their weapon upgrades to complete, they decided to check out the town and first went to the docks. That is where they met the Liveship Vivacia and her captain, Captain Everett. The group is familiar with liveships and Jin knows that captains die on their ships so their memories are passed onto the ship itself, so each ship is a collection of memories of captains past. She also has other, stranger memories, ones where she is flying on the wind, dreams of seeing cities from miles above, memories and dreams that don't feel real. They also talk about how the trading life is tough lately, with lots of ships being lost to more frequent serpent attacks.

The group left the busy docks and headed to scope out Lord Carsten Rhyne's manor. Axin approached the guard looking for work and convinced the guard leader to hire him on the spot. They did not seem too worried about trusting him since in order to take this job he needs to be on campus 6 days a week, working 12 hours and resting for 12 hours, so they will have plenty of eyes on him. His first shift starts at 8. The group waited until this shift and Axin was provided a walkthrough of the entire manor, including the basement where the vault is supposedly located. But there was no vault, just a wine cellar, which seemed odd given one quarter of their guards are just guarding wine.

Axin's first shift was the outdoor shift where he gave all this info to Harlan. While his hour at the second floor, Axin found the necklace that Lord Rhyne had put away and found that it was a key. Axin pocketed the key and did the next 3 hours of his shift, bringing the group to midnight where they are tasked with the plan. With someone on the inside, at which point does it make sense to infiltrate the manor, get to the basement, get past the stationed guard, find the vault, retrieve the item, and get back to Nyx? Physical distraction? Magical distraction? Pure stealth? Brute force? The group must decide how to move forward, get in the good graces of the king, and figure out what all of this means.

### Session 5 - The Infiltration

It's midnight and Axin is at his station at the front gate. He has two things in his pockets, a key to Lord Rhyne's estate, and a key on a necklace he found in the master bedroom. Axin speaks with his group and unlocks the side door for them before heading to the basement for his next watch. Dax was lookout, Harlan and Marcel snuck in, went to the basement and found Axin guarding a wine cellar. Harlan detected a magical device that looked like a wine bottle and upon moving it a door opened. The next door looked like a vault, and with the key were able to get into the vault. They picked the lock containing the box they were sent to get, but also took an axe, a hammer, a scarf, a locket, and a bag of gems, cleaning out the vault of everything magical. With their job finished, and 30 minutes until shift change, the three of them threw the vault key into the stew and snuck out the same way they came in. They immediately went to Nyx's abandoned building where they are shown a secret entryway into the castle and find out the box was surprisingly empty. They are shown their way to quarters where they find food, shelter, a bath, and an 11-year-old boy named Kristofer to wait on them. Everyone falls asleep while Dax sharpens his new axe in front of the fire, thinking of his father, his mother he never met, but most importantly, he gets lost remembering his father's tales of dragon riders.

### Session 6 - Intercepting Rhyne

Waking in the morning Nyx is already in the castle room with Kristofer and tells the group they need to intercept Lord Carsten Rhyne's carriage, kill the guards, and bring him back for questioning. They eat breakfast, Dax reads some of the books Kristofer has given him and learns about dragons and dragonbloods, eventually go to the king's stables and kennels, get some horses and feed for the trip, and head out. They get a bit lost on the way, but eventually make it to the spot pointed out by Nyx. Once there they wait several hours until a carriage comes down the path. The driver looks asleep at first, but then the party realizes they are dead and from inside the carriage a bandit archer shoots Dax and a battle ensues. The PCs win the battle with a few scrapes and bruises, but nothing serious, and end up stabilizing one of the near-dead bandits. They also find out near the end that the bandits cannot give up because they will be killed by Rhaenard, the second in line to be prince. After searching they find Rhyne's personal ledger, linking his finances to Rhaenard and a mysterious foreign source. They also find a medallion on the bandits that is common in Zephandor, the mountain kingdom.

### Session 7 - Bandit Battle

The group was ambushed by bandits, but being prepared they easily were able to put them down. During the battle some wolves interrupted, but Harlan was able to talk them down. The session ended with the team figuring out what to do next.

### Session 8 - Healing Up

Corona Eclipsa gathered themselves after the battle, swung the injured bandit over a horse, and headed back to Everthrone. On the way they saw a campsite in the distance and decided not to explore, when Jin fell off his horse. Jin was contacted by his god Elos, whose real name is Soryan. Soryan asked Jin to be his champion, reminding Soryan of his first champion, a long forgotten slave who fought for the well being of the other slaves. He immediately returned and his robes glowed as he received some Cloistered Robes from the gods.

Corona Eclipsa brought the ledger, Zephandor amulets, and long dead bandit back to Nyx. She said she would bring it to the king, which started a week of downtime. Axin and Marcel earned a bit of gold working at the Thirsty Minstrel, Jin searched for anything about the catalyst and the old gods, Dax read about the old histories of dragons and dragon society, and Harlan found a book about the Piebald Prince, a king from a hundred years ago who once embraced beast magic.

After downtime Nyx informed Corona Eclipsa that they would be needed that morning to listen as extra ears for the crown. They are to hide in the walls and catch anything they can from the scheduled meetings. They are to hear from the Princess of Zephandor, a boy from Iron Hollow, a man to discuss details on the Harvest Festival next month, and a bandit recently caught and is currently being held in the gaol.

### Session 9 - Secret Meetings

Corona Eclipsa was asked to listen to various meetings with the king from behind the walls. The first was Princess Serel Aiuvarin from Zephandor, where she offered a union with the Vaelorians via marriage. The second was a dock boy who ran from Iron Hollow who described the fall and how the entire town has turned to animals. The third meeting was with Nellis Corwin who went over preparations for the Harvest Festival next month. The last scheduled meeting was with the bandit that ran from the players while bringing Ames to Everthrone. After the meetings, King Aldric confronted Rhaenard about the death of Carsten Rhyne. Rhaenard brushed it off and blamed the bandits and that it was clearly Zephandor that killed him.

After the meetings the king asked to meet the group and decide on which thread to pull on first. Before deciding Dax tried to get more info from the bandit, but was laughed at. Harlan went back later that night, got the bandit a little drunk, and learned that he didn't know exactly who he worked for, but that he had heard Rhaenard's name.

Nyx came back to see what the plan was and Corona Eclipsa told her that they would look into Iron Hollow before heading to Zephandor as representatives of the king.

### Session 10 - Investigate Iron Hollow

Corona Eclipsa was tasked to investigate what is happening to Iron Hollow and all the other towns along the coast. Before heading out Harlan heard a voice in his head who called him `packless`, talked with Garrik about the dogs, and was given a stern warning to learn to control this power, strongly suggesting that he understands what is going on. The group got on their horses and set off to Iron Hollow.

While there they found a town that was empty. They investigated a few shops and the barracks, but as they went further into the town they heard loud scratching, before a very large group of townsfolk turned animal ran out and attacked them in the square. It was a tough battle, but they ended up killing the entire town. They found the same Zephandorian amulets on many of the bodies, nobody is left alive, and the bodies they did find looked like they had been eaten.

### Session 11 - Back from Iron Hollow

Corona Eclipsa finished up their investigation of Iron Hollow and returned to find Nyx waiting for them at the gates. They were immediately taken to Prince Damion's tower where he was using weave magic to control storms, some of which they saw first hand at Iron Hollow. They were then tasked from Damion to head to Zehphirion, the capital of Zephandor, as his envoy. In the meantime there were several downtime activities each character worked on. Dax found his missing horse and despite it being visibly angry for being put in that scenario, seemed to forgive him. He spent most of his time finding and reading various books on dragons. Jin spent time foraging and studying the few books he found on the old gods. Harlan spent his time reading about beast magic and while going around town, found a captured wolf who called him `packless` and convinced Harlan to set him free. Marcel researched the catalyst while trying to improve his athleticism.

After a few days the team left in a group of about 100, ordered to listen to the lead guard during travel. Sir Gery told them they were to be used as capable guards for the troupe. During the first night a fire was seen in the distance and Gery ordered the group to investigate. There they found symbols of a dragon long since dead as well as the word `silence`. They went back, retrieved a couple of guards, Jayne and Gym, to head through the cave first. They made their way through and were coming back to report what they found.

### Session 12 - Silent Dungeon

Corona Eclipsa has been tasked by the king and prince to go to Zephandor as liaison and treat with the mountain kingdom court. The first day of travel was uneventful, however they were tasked the first night to investigate a fire off in the distance. There they found a mysterious cave, to which they went back to get some reinforcements. After sending the traveling guards into the cave, they returned saying the place is empty. Inside the group found that the cave was actually not a cave, but a purposefully built underground dungeon. After investigating they found an odd set of statues that started to glow in counterclockwise order as people stepped in the room, and stopped during any sound. Allowing all of the statues to glow created a magical vision of dragon keepers extracting the soul of a dragon and trapping it.

Once the group exited the room the doors suddenly closed as a monster of an armed guard with a single glowing eye woke up and battled with the group as the room itself wrought havoc on everyone, causing mass confusion. Seeing this, Marcel used a spell to determine how to stop it and found that there were similar runes from the statues on the coffin. Touching those in the same order as the statues glowed, counterclockwise, they were able to turn off the madness-causing room and dealt with the armed guard.

Exhausted from the battle the group found a magical breastplate, of which Axin immediately equipped. The door back the way they came is locked closed, so the only way forward is to explore deeper into the dungeon.

### Session 13 - Silent Dragon

Corona Eclipsa, on the way to the Zephandor kingdom, were tasked to investigate a mysterious fire in the distance on the first night of travel. There they found a magical dungeon, protected by the spirit of the dragon Vaeltharyn and his rider. Locked inside with these monsters they were forced to battle, narrowly escaping with their lives, but leaving with some mysterious boons. Axin was able to find a Wyrmbound Breastplate that allows him to cast `silence` around himself once per day, a cloak was found but Marcel was unable to identify it, a masterfully crafted necklace of dragon wings surrounded by obsidian, various potions, and some vials of the mysterious silver. The necklace has not been attempted to be identified, but was given to Dax. The session ended as the group collected the overwhelmed guards as they left the dungeon.

### Session 14 - The Trip to Greythorn

Last we left off, Corona Eclipsa had been on the way to the Zephandor kingdom, helping lead a caravan of travelers. They had a brief investigation stop at an odd cave where they found the silent remains of the dragon Vaeltheryn and cleaned the dungeon of his presence. That night, seemingly propelled by the magic well they touched in the dungeon, everyone was plagued by oddities through the night.

Marcel had dreams of Rhaenard treating with Princess Serel, the princess and Prince Dorian being wed, the king sick in his bed, Prince Dorian traversing through mountain cold as he finds large carved statues of dragons, occasionally with their riders, and finally the woman from his previous dream, masked in darkness. He awoke with pain all along his back.

Harlan, in secret, began a conversation with the rescued wolf who had been following the group. A few days of freedom and the wolf is already looking better than the starved beast with matted fur he rescued. He gave a gift of a freshly killed rabbit, told Harlan his name is Nighteyes, and left to presumably find a place to rest for the night.

Jin's night was restless as he saw Soryan at the edge of his sight. Running towards him brought the god no closer, until finally he began falling. Looking up, Jin saw a look of disappointment in his eyes, but whether that was disappointment in Jin or himself is yet to be seen.

Finally, Dax had dreams of dragons. Of soaring through the air, a bond with his mount unlike anything he has ever experienced. The dragon circled a large city surrounded by mountains, forest, and swamp before landing near a large well full of the silver liquid. The dragon drank from this and Dax could feel it warm him as well, rejuvenating his body and soul before drifting into an even deeper sleep.

After a long night and early morning, Corona Eclipsa woke to the pattering of steady rain. They helped pack the tents and set out toward Greythorn, helping where they could to get the caravan there before nightfall. Arriving in the late afternoon, the group first went to buy some warmer clothes from the general store, and then perused the selection of books at the local bookstore. After inquiring, they found all of the books to be extraordinarily expensive, and while Harlan was able to do a bit of reading, Dax took the approach of threatening the owner and forced him to trade the book he took from the king's library for another dragon book. Marcel was also tasked with finding another catalyst book and gave the general guidance that it is located in the northern Vraegari islands.

The group headed to the inn where they got some drinks, a bit of food, and headed to bed for the night. While preparing for sleep, two things occurred. Dax realized you accidentally threw a second book in with your first. Looking at the cover you see a half-orc, heroically looking at his sword in the sunlight, while at his feet is a young woman, longingly looking up. The book looks to be named `Tusk Love`. Jin noticed that Marcel seems to have tattoos along his back, ones you did not realize he had before.

### Session 15 - Greythorn to Zephirion

Last we left off, Corona Eclipsa had left the small town of Greythorn for their last leg of a trip from Everthrone to Zephirion. Arriving later that evening, the group discovered a surreal scene of manmade buildings flowing effortlessly into the natural wood and stone of the surrounding forest and mountains. After a long day of travel they were given an opportunity to visit the local bath house and get cleaned up. They spent the better part of two hours getting massages, mud baths, pampered, and finally getting a hot bath where they stared at each other in silence for the better part of 30 minutes before Marcel asked, "Can we be done with this bath?" and Geoffrey the attendant happily obliged.

They then proceeded to meet with the royal Aiuvarin family: Princess Serel, Prince Kaelith, Queen Lirael, and King Therion, but upon entering the dining area they were surprised to see the family had already started dining and already had a guest, Prince Rhaenard. The prince, clearly in his cups, boasted about Corona Eclipsa and how efficient they are at killing, destroying an entire town in an afternoon. The king, curious about this tale, asked for a recounting and was overall satisfied with their answers, though he seemed visibly wary of the group. He did, however, suggest that one or two of the group be given an opportunity to show off those skills in the ring against their current hand-to-hand fighting champion, as part of their entertainment for the following evening. Both Jin and Dax agreed to this.

The next day the group spent the hours reading, crafting potions, and exploring the city before heading to the castle for dinner. There they were greeted with a fine meal and at the end of the meal the central area was cleared for the first part of the evening's festivities: hand-to-hand combat. Harlan has 50 gold riding on this and Marcel has 100.

### Session 16 - Dinner in Zephirion

Last we left off, Corona Eclipsa had finished their dinner at the royal Zephiorion castle and were the subjects of that evening's entertainment, fist fighting General Vaern Corvath in front of the entire royal family. The general, well known for his combat prowess, with some effort was able to dispatch Dax and Jin. As a reward for being a good sport, Dax was given a ring of underwater breathing. After the fight the band started playing music, the guests began dancing, and Marcel was invited on stage for a bit of a musical duel, which nobody really won.

At the end of the night the group was approached by Princess Serel, where she offered to escort them back to their rooms. It was there that they found Prince Kaelith and a host of guards waiting for them. There, Corona Eclipsa discussed with the prince and princess of Zephandor the seriousness of the request to Prince Dorian for peace through marriage, and that they both know of the plot to kill the prince, but despite that they still want peace through this marriage.

The royals left the group in their room alone for the better part of an hour, discussing what to do next and who to talk to. Corona Eclipsa really wanted to talk with this princess more. It was at that moment that a knock came at the door, asking Harlan to meet with the princess and leading him to the bathhouse. After getting into one of the baths, out came Prince Rhaenard who proceeded to tell him that he will do anything to be king, that he was the one who killed Rhye because he was working with someone powerful, and that he will destroy Corona Eclipsa one by one. Nothing personal, someone has to be the first, and the session ended with Harlan taking his first breath of warm bath water.

### Session 17 - Rhaenard's Aftermath

Last we left off, Harlan was left to drown in the bathhouse by Rhaenard. Hearing a desperate call, Nighteyes ran as fast as he could and burst through the main window. Watching this from across the street, Marcel followed the animal into the bathhouse where he found Harlan lying outside a bath, not breathing, with a wolf over him. Marcel was able to stabilize Harlan before city guards came in, investigating the assumed break-in. They were questioned, but they determined they were to be brought to Princess Serel and Prince Kaelith. Upon arrival, the rest of Corona Eclipsa was retrieved and brought to the main hall to meet Marcel and Harlan. After some time, Serel enters the room and tells everyone the prince has been found dead and to arrest Corona Eclipsa.

The next morning the princess came to talk to the group in their very nice "cells." The guards had found poison and stolen goods in their possession, so she wanted answers. During this process they were able to find the guard, identify him, and determine that he was a simple grunt looking for more pay, and successfully convince the princess that they were not responsible for her brother's death. The king also wanted to speak to the group, seemingly less interested in the death of his son, but more interested in the fact that the group came to his kingdom and stole from other guests. Angry and sad, King Therion suggested that they head back to Everthrone with their message of peace, Daxon sending a message via bird to Dorian in Everthrone telling him these details.

That evening they had a somber meal, remembering Prince Kaelith, his impulsiveness, but also his decisiveness. Corona Eclipsa was especially somber as they realized that all of their gold had been taken.
